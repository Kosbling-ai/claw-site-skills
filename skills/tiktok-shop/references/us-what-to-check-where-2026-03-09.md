## Notes

- ⚠️ **LIVE & video analytics** may show no data if no LIVE streams have been conducted
- ⚠️ **Compliance center** returns 404 — use Account health for compliance info
- **PEAKS Framework** is TikTok's seller performance methodology — see Analytics → Shop analytics
- Many pages require **hover** on left sidebar to reveal submenus
